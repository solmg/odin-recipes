Hot Dog

Description

Delicious and quick snack from the USA. The Hot Dog is an iconic savoury snack which is very very easy to prepare.

  • Cooking time: 3 min
  • Difficulty: Very Easy
  • Number of ingredients: 4

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Sausage
  • Mustard
  • Ketchupp

Steps

  1. Cut the hot dog bun in half but only half way down.
  2. Put the sausage in the microwave for 30 seconds.
  3. Put the sausage inside the bun.
  4. Top it with ketchupp and mustard for the ultimate taste.
