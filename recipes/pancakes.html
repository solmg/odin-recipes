Banana Pancakes

Description

This top-rated banana pancake recipe is easy to make and perfect for a sweet and quick breakfast. Learn how to make the best banana pancakes ever in just 15 minutes!

  • Cooking time: 15 min
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Number of ingredients: 8

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Mix your dry ingredients (flour,sugar,baking powder, salt) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, vegetable oil, bananas) in another bowl.
  2. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until a smooth batter is formed.
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto an oiled pan and cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula and cook for another few minutes.
  4. Serve with maple syrup or honey and bananas.
